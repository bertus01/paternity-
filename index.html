Welcome to the paternity- project !

The package include functions to calculate composite paterity index for paternity testing. The package can only be used for research purpose (no legal or diagnostic purpose), as such implication was not tested or used. For confirmation purpose suggest to use other software along with this package. The method is used and discussed in the following manuscript:

Rosyara U.R., A. M. Sebolt, C. Peace, and A.F. Iezzoni (2013) Identification of the Paternal Parent of ‘Bing’ Sweet Cherry and Confirmation of Descendants Using Single Nucleotide Polymorphism Markers, J. AMER. SOC. HORT. SCI. 139(2):148–156. 2014.

Beside downloading here from github, the package can also be installed from R-forge:

install.packages("plantbreeding",repos="http://r-forge.r-project.org")
